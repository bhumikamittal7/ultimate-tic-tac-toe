Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe

A strategic twist on the classic game!

How to Play:

  • The game consists of 9 smaller tic-tac-toe boards arranged in a 3x3 grid
  • Your move in a small board determines which board your opponent must play in next
  • Win a small board by getting three in a row to claim that section
  • Win the overall game by claiming three small boards in a row
  • If sent to a won or full board, you can play anywhere!
